Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does the process work?

After purchasing a plan, you'll receive a detailed questionnaire about your travel preferences, interests, and any specific requirements. Our travel experts will then create a personalized itinerary based on your responses, which you'll receive within 3-5 business days.

Do you book flights and accommodations?

No, we focus on creating personalized itineraries and recommendations. You'll be responsible for making your own bookings based on our recommendations, giving you full control over your travel plans and budget.

Can I request changes to my itinerary?

Yes! The number of revisions depends on your chosen plan. Explorer includes 1 round of revisions, Adventurer includes 2 rounds, and Globetrotter includes unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend reaching out at least 3-4 weeks before your trip to ensure we have enough time to create your perfect itinerary. For complex trips or peak travel seasons, 6-8 weeks in advance is ideal.
